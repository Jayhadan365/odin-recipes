Tacos

Description

Tacos are a family favorite in my household, so what better way to spread some joy than to show you how we do it where I'm from! This recipe is easy to follow and will leave you with flavorful, delicious tacos that will have you coming back for more week after week!

What you will need:

  • Soft or hard taco shells; either will work
  • One pound of ground beef (this recipe uses 73% lean beef)
